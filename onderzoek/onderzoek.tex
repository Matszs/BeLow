\section{Introduction}
% =============================================================================
For the past several years, the changing demographic in the Netherlands has raised many concerns. The growing number of elderly people living alone and the question of how to care for the ageing population have caused a surge of technological developments aimed specifically at this targed audience. However, even if a demand for ambient intelligence based systems such as these, the market does not quite seem to take off in the way that most developers hope for.
